    How Much Do Cam Girls Make?

    Short answer: a lot. It's not a secret to anyone that cam models are earning a fortune, otherwise you probably wouldn't be reading this post right now.


    If you're planning to become a cam model it's perfectly understandable that one of the first questions crossing your mind is: How much do cam girls make?. But since this type of work is flexible by nature and revenue depends on multiple factors, it is really hard to give a typical number. Based on my experiences though, a new cam girl who's not too shy and has the the right equipment can easily make around $40-50 per hour. It's clearly up to you how much work you're willing to put into camming. With a little experience and a bigger fanbase it's possible to push these numbers up to $150-200 within a few months or a year (mix of hard work, creativity-skill and luck). And lets not forget about the other revenue sources such as selling your photos and videos, Snapchat (nude snap), selling used clothes, contests and extra prizes on various cam sites. But generally speaking, expect the income to be low ($1500-2000) in the first month or two until you figure it out where does the traffic come from and what earns the most tokens.

    What are the main factors?
    Consistent Schedule

    As I mentioned several times in the How To Become A Cam Girl Guide, building a solid fanbase / followerbase should be your main focus if you want to achieve a steady income. Consistent income requires a consisten schedule, this is also important because the longer you were online the bigger is your chance to make more money since this will increase your chatroom ranking on most sites (not to mention you even get paid an hourly bonus if you manage to rank high enough + the possibility of winning a contest!).

    So for success you definitely need to treat camming as a business or serious job and stay organized, otherwise if you're planning to go online 1-2 times a week for a few hours then don't even start.

    Good Mood & Positive Attitude

    Never look too desperate for money or just bored, don't forget that most people who visit these websites want to emotionally connect and share. Be nice and interract with your viewers (it's not all about dropping down those panties) or you'll get a bad reputation fairly quickly which leads to low regular viewers. Yes, this requires a lot of mental strength and experience to be able to do this consistently.

    Good Looks

    The better looking the performer is, the easier is to start making money in the beginning. But this really shouldn't get anyone discouraged, there is an audiance for every shape, size and gender. On the long-term what's really matters is your personality and your the relationsip with your fanbase.

    Things The Model Is Willing To Do

    The more things you are willing to do, the more you'll earn. With the tons of fetishes out there, be prepared for many "weird" requests. On most sites of course you can set your goal tables, set the rules and ask the viewers to act according to these, which will get easier with an established foollowerbase.

    Final Words

    I hope with these insights you manage to put together the answer you were looking for. A cam girl's earning potential depends on these factors, we can't just look at a few examples and set those as a standard, because maybe there are things you'll do much better than them and earn more.
