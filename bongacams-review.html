Tips usually depend on the show you give, teasing a little can help a lot.

    What are "full private" and spy shows? Users can request a private show (1-on-1) and if you accept it, the client will be charged per minute instead tipping tokkens. Spy shows, also known as "fake privates" which means you can broadcast the same private show on multiple websites for extra profit, this is called cam-splitting and it's 100% allowed on sites such as BongaCams and Chaturbate. The difference between private and full private shows is that full private shows cannot be spied on or be cam-splitted, it is only performed for one person exclusively on one specific camming site but obviously you can charge a lot more for this special treat (45 tokens / minute, up to $10 per minute).

    Features & Usability

    Strong DMCA protection and onsite lawyer: This is definitely one of their strong points and unique features. If your show was recorded or any content was taken without your premision and uploaded to third-party websites, you can just contact the support and they'll take steps immediately.

    Pay out in BTC: Another unique feature I haven't seen on any other cam site. You can easily withdraw funds through bitcoin apps and bitcoin machine, but only choose this option if you're familiar with cryptocurrency.

    Contests: Contests are a brilliant opportunity to make some extra money and they are held twice a week. The 100 best models get a prize in every contest, 1st place gets $1000, prize for 2nd is $500, and $300 for 3rd place. If you finished between 4th and 100th place you'll get $100 or $15 based on ranking. The awesome thing about contests is that you don't really have to do anything special to participate, just try to earn as much tokens you can and keep working hard.

    CamScore: It is calculated based on two main factors: how long you've been live and token earnings. The CamScore's purpose is to determine your listing, so the model with the highest CamScore will be on the top of the sort order.

    Mobile App: You can just simply download and install it on your smartphone and start broadcasting from anywhere! This is awesome feature for those of you who don't have a high definiton webcam but still want to get into camming.

    Support
