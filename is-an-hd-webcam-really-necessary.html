    Is An HD Webcam Really Necessary?


    In 2018 you'll definitely need to broadcast in at least 720p if you're serious about camming and want to be succesful. Those viewers who don't mind the low quality are most of the time just "freeloaders", you can't build a fanbase if 90% of your viewers are from this group. So with a low quality webcam don't expect to attract the highest paying clients, it's not impossible to make money, but the difference in revenue is massive.

    Alternative Solutions

    If you don't want to spend on a HD Webcam or just want to jump right into camming, there are still some options left for you.

    Using A Real Camera

    Using a bridge camera or dSLR and a capture card is one of the best ways to get better image quality. Just don't forget you will also need a tripod and an external microphone with this solution.

    Download OBS, get yourself a tripod, a mic, some photography umbrellas, light sources, and you're guranteed to have a better stream quality than 95% of the performers out there.

    Turn Your Smartphone Into A Webcam

    This is the least ideal solution, but if you have an Android Phone or iPhone and really want to get into camming asap, then nothing can stop you.
