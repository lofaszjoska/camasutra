    Clips, Videos & Pictures

     

    Having multiple sources of income will help to build your revenue. If you've decided to become a cam model or you're already one, I advise to consider these alternates as well since they don't require too much extra work.

    This includes selling photos, clips and videos, and the options you have depends on the websites (for example you can offer paid memberships or sell these clips / photos piecemeal, etc.)

    Networks like Chaturbate and BongaCams have this feature inbuilt, and by setting it up you have nothing to lose (especially profitable with an already established fanbase). But you're not limited to keep your content exclusively on these camming networks, there are some websites which specialize for selling it.

     
    Some examples:

     

    • ManyVids - Probably the most well-known and popular choice. The focus is on the models, and the content is more "standard". Niche videos / fetish pics and videos are sold at a higher price.
    • NiteFlirt - The most popular phone-sex platform out there. Professional and well organized, their only downsite is the low payout.
    • Custome4u - You can sell clips just like on ManyVids, mostly fetish related content.
    • SkyPrivate - Basicaly it's a Skype plug-in, the client is charged per minute (so it's like a private show on any camming site)
    • SnapCash - Many models include this on their camming profiles, you can charge for some photos.
